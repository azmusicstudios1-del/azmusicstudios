Private One-on-One

ENGINEERING
COURSES

AZ Music Studios offers private, one-on-one courses for anyone who would like to learn everything about engineering. Whether you're just a beginner wanting to start with the basics, or someone who already has some knowledge and wants to push further — this is perfect for all.

School for engineering can get very expensive, and a lot of the time the courses just aren't that interesting. With AZ's classes, we focus on the stuff that actually matters and cut out all the filler.

If you're just starting out, we'll build from the ground up — covering the basics of Logic and Ableton, simple recording and mixing techniques, and working our way up. If you're already comfortable with the basics, we'll jump straight into advanced mixing and mastering. Classes move at a pace that works for you.

Curriculum

WHAT YOU
WILL LEARN

How to use Logic and Ableton — the industry standard DAWs for recording, mixing, and mastering.

Proper recording techniques.

What microphones and types of gear are best for your home studio.

How to use different plugins to improve the quality of your music:

  • EQing
  • Compression
  • De-essing
  • Saturation
  • Reverb & Delay
  • Pitch correction / tuning
  • And much more…

Mixing techniques that will make your music sound professional.

Top-tier mastering techniques.

Professional advice on how to get your engineering career started — finding clients, setting up your studio, building your brand.

Live in-person mixing tutorials.

Beat-making and music production techniques.

And much more…
