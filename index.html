Our Story

ABOUT
AZ MUSIC

Founded in Montreal in 2016 by producer, songwriter, and engineer Anthony Zappavigna, AZ Music Studios was built on a single belief: every artist deserves a space where their creative vision can thrive without compromise.

From emerging artists cutting their first demo to established acts putting the finishing touches on a full album, we've been the trusted partner for Montreal's most ambitious musicians.

  • From Neve pre-amps and compressors to Neumann U87 microphones and UAD output gear
  • Acoustically treated iso booth and control room
  • Over 20 million streams worldwide
